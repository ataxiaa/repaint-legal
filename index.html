10. PRIVACY POLICY

Repaint collects only the minimum data necessary to operate the platform.

What we collect: email address, chosen username and display name, optional profile information you provide (bio, social links), content you upload, and in-app interactions (likes, follows, messages).

What we do not collect: real name, phone number, address, government ID, location data, device contacts, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

Camera & Gallery: Access is requested only when you choose to upload a photo. Only the selected image is accessed. The App does not scan or store your gallery.

Image Processing & Face Data: When you upload a photo for AI artistic processing, the image is temporarily transmitted to Ataxia's secure server infrastructure solely for artistic style transfer processing. No facial recognition, biometric data extraction, facial mapping, or face identification is performed at any point. The original photo is never stored on our servers — it is permanently discarded immediately after the style transfer process is complete. Only the processed output image is saved to your account on Supabase. Processed images are permanently deleted when you delete the post or your account. Image processing is performed exclusively for the purpose of applying the selected artistic filter and for no other purpose. This data is not shared with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data broker services.

On-Device NSFW Detection: Mature content classification is performed entirely on-device using a TFLite model. No image data is sent to external servers for classification purposes. This process does not involve facial recognition or biometric data of any kind.

Data storage: All user data and processed images are stored via Supabase (EU-Central, Frankfurt), operating under GDPR-compliant infrastructure.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing. You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile > Settings.
